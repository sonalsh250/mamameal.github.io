TYPES OF CUISINE

Gujrati Cuisine

Gujrati thali consists of rotli, kadhi, rice and shaak with different combinations of vegetables and spices.

Maharashtrian Cuisine

Maharashtrian food consists of dal, rice, roti, bhaji, poori, salad, chutney, dry vegetable curry and puranpoli.

Jain Cuisine

Jain thali consists of vegetables, squash, beans, peas, fruits and lettuce and excludes onions and garlic, root vegetables.

South Indian Cuisine

South Indian food consists of dosa, idli, rasam, sambar, poha, upma, chana dal chutney and mysore pak for sweets.
